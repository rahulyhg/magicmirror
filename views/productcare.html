PRODUCT CARE

Every piece of Magic Mirror jewellery is unique, with proper care your jewellery pieces will retain their beauty and character for generations to come. Below are some care guidelines to help you keep your jewellery sparkling:

Beautiful Stones

Protect the jewellery from being scratched, and from scratching other pieces. Remove your jewels when swimming and gardening so you don't lose them or damage them on rough surfaces.

Silver

As with most precious metals, in time silver will oxidize. Properly maintained silver actually improves with age and it develops a good polish when treated well. Occasionally wiping your silver pieces with a silver polishing cloth will keep them bright and shiny.

Gold Plating

To maintain its bright appearance your gold plated earrings may need to be re-plated once every two to three year, bangles, rings and necklaces will depend on how often you wear them. Remove jewellery when washing hands, showering and sleeping. .

No Harsh Chemicals

Salt water and harsh chemicals such as chlorine bleach or detergents can damage your precious jewellery. Hairspray, perfume & other such chemicals can also cause dullness if not regularly cleaned off, and it's best not to shower in your jewellery.

Secure Settings

Settings should be checked by a jewellery once a year. Although we take great care when setting a stone, constant wear can cause damage to settings particularly claw settings which can wear down over time or be caught on material causing them to bend. Cracked and lost stones are not guaranteed.

Warranty

Magic Mirror jewellery carries a warranty. This warranty covers only manufacturing faults and we will at own discretion replace or repair the faulty/damaged product.
